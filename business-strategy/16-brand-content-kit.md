**Mục đích:** Đảm bảo voice + visual + content consistency across all channels. 3 founder-voice + 1 freelance không bị brand fragmentation.

## 1. Brand positioning

### 1.1 1-line positioning

> **"AI consulting & products from a real LLMOps shop — built on production runs, not vibe."**

Việt: *"Đội ngũ AI thật làm AI thật cho doanh nghiệp Việt — eval, cost track, ship được, không nói suông."*

### 1.2 Promise

3 thứ chúng tôi luôn deliver:
1. **Tested** — mọi sản phẩm/service có eval framework, không phải "trust me"
2. **Affordable** — pricing transparent file 10, không upsell trap
3. **Vietnamese-aware** — content + delivery hiểu thị trường VN, không paste từ US playbook

### 1.3 What we are NOT

- ❌ Vibe consultancy ("AI sẽ thay đổi mọi thứ" rỗng)
- ❌ Hype merchant (auto-tweet về model mới)
- ❌ Generic outsource agency (chỉ làm theo brief khách)
- ❌ Pure SaaS without service (mất touch khách hàng)
- ❌ MMO scammer hype ("$10K trong 30 ngày" rỗng)

## 2. Voice guide — 3 voices co-exist

### 2.1 Voice A — Technical Authority (CEO)

**Channels:** YouTube long-form, Substack, GitHub, X EN

**Tone:**
- Hands-on, code-first
- Numbers and benchmarks > opinions
- Show work (commits, screenshots, before/after)
- Bilingual (VN-first, EN reach)
- Critical of vibe but respectful

**Voice samples:**

✅ DO:
- "Cut $5K/month on Anthropic by routing 80% calls to Haiku. Here's the decision tree."
- "Why we chose NBEATS over Prophet for sparse retail data — 3 datasets benchmarked."
- "Eval framework v0.5 shipped. 3 lessons from running on 50 production agents."

❌ DON'T:
- "AI is the future of business" (empty)
- "Game-changing breakthrough" (hype)
- "Mind-blowing demo" (cliché)
- "Revolutionizing X" (overpromise)

### 2.2 Voice B — Business B2B (P3)

**Channels:** LinkedIn VN, niche audit reports, founder community DMs

**Tone:**
- Mature, business-context-aware
- Bridges tech ↔ outcome
- Vietnamese business culture-fluent (formal but warm)
- ROI-focused, không tech-talk-down

**Voice samples:**

✅ DO:
- "3PL anh chị đang dùng 12 nhân viên cho customs docs? Đây là use case AI tiết kiệm $15K/năm — đã verify ở 1 client."
- "Sau 2 tuần Discovery, chúng tôi recommend NÊN trì hoãn project AI này vì lý do X. Đây là báo cáo."

❌ DON'T:
- "Disruption" / "transformation" (vague)
- Tech jargon mà DM không hiểu (LLM, RAG, fine-tune)
- Quá cao siêu ("Hãy mơ lớn cùng AI...")

### 2.3 Voice C — MMO Energetic (freelance creator)

**Channels:** TikTok, Threads, FB groups (VN MMO niche)

**Tone:**
- High-energy, hook-driven
- Concrete numbers ("kiếm $X trong Y ngày")
- Show, don't tell — screen records, real results
- Call-to-action mạnh

**Voice samples:**

✅ DO:
- "Tôi build agent AI làm 50 video TikTok/tháng cho khách. Đây là setup..."
- "Không phải prompt nào cũng kiếm tiền. 5 prompt tôi dùng hằng ngày..."
- "Stop trả $200/tháng cho ChatGPT — đây là alternative miễn phí + better."

❌ DON'T:
- "Get rich quick" promises
- Trading signal / financial advice
- Fake screenshots / numbers
- Spam comments với link

### 2.4 Voice rules cross-team

- ❌ KHÔNG mix voice trong 1 channel (CEO không post MMO hook trên YouTube)
- ✅ Cross-promote OK ("Business team chúng tôi serve B2B")
- ✅ Bio mỗi channel ghi rõ voice scope
- ✅ Brand color/font consistent (visual identity §3)

## 7. Brand voice — Vietnamese specifics

### 7.1 Tone register

- **Default:** Tôi/em (humble), bạn/anh chị (respectful)
- **Tránh:** "tao/mày" (too casual), "quý khách" (too distant)
- **Mixing EN-VN:** OK với technical terms ("eval", "prompt", "RAG"), NOT OK với general words

### 7.2 Common pitfalls VN audience

- ❌ "Cách Mạng AI" / "Đột phá" — overhyped, audience numb
- ❌ "Make money online" without showing real money — audience cynical
- ✅ Specific numbers (giấc mơ → $5K/mo, "tiết kiệm" → $15K/year)
- ✅ Real screenshots (anonymized) > stock photos
- ✅ Vietnamese cultural context (Tết, doanh nghiệp gia đình, etc.)

### 7.3 Inclusive language

- Audience trẻ + giới tính đa dạng → dùng "bạn" thay "anh em"
- B2B + tuổi trung niên → "anh chị" thay "các bạn"
- Match channel:
  - TikTok/MMO: "bạn" / "fen"
  - LinkedIn B2B: "anh chị" / "quý vị"
  - YouTube technical: "bạn" or "các bạn dev"

## 9. Crisis comms playbook

### 9.1 Negative review / public criticism

| Severity | Response time | Channel |
|---|---|---|
| Single negative comment | < 24h, neutral acknowledge | Same channel |
| Going viral negatively | < 4h, P3 + CEO align then respond | Same + new long-form post |
| Customer publicly unhappy | < 1h DM, then public response within 24h | Both |

**Rules:**
- ✅ Acknowledge first
- ✅ Apologize if wrong
- ✅ Explain context
- ✅ Offer specific fix
- ❌ Never delete (Streisand effect)
- ❌ Never argue publicly
- ❌ Never blame customer

### 9.2 OSS issue / accusations

- Code of Conduct violation report → P2 + CEO review within 48h
- Security disclosure → CEO acknowledges within 24h, fix + advisory within 14 days
- IP accusation → consult lawyer immediately, don't respond until counsel
