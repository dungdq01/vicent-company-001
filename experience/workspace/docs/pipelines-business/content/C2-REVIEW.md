# C2 — Review

> CEO/founder polish + fact-check before publish.

**Owner**: CEO · **Agent**: Human + R-CONTENT (revise loop)

---

## Engine Orchestration

| Step | Owner | Action |
|---|---|---|
| C2.1 | Human (CEO/founder) | 15-30 min red-pen pass per article; 5 min per social. |
| C2.2 | Human | Fact-check stats, claims, citations. |
| C2.3 | R-CONTENT | Apply edits, regenerate if > 30% rewrite needed. |
| C2.4 | Human | Approve "ready to publish" tag. |

## Definition of Done
- ✅ Edit ratio recorded (target ≤ 30% v1, ≤ 15% v1.2)
- ✅ Fact-check pass — every stat verified
- ✅ Voice consistency with anchor pieces
- ✅ Plagiarism check < 15% similarity to corpus
- ✅ Image / hero asset attached if article

## Failure Modes
- **Rubber-stamp review**: same edit ratio every time → spot-check by 2nd founder.
- **Re-write loop > 2**: scrap topic or escalate to CEO direct write.
- **Publish without review**: hard block; ready-to-publish tag mandatory.
