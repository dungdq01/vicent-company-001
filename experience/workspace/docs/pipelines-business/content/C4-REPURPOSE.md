# C4 — Repurpose

> 1 article → 7 derivatives. Within 24h of publish.

**Owner**: CEO · **Agents**: R-MKT (plan) + R-CONTENT (execute)
**Canonical**: [`@../../../../../business-strategy/16-brand-content-kit.md:364`](../../../../../business-strategy/16-brand-content-kit.md)

## The 1→7 Pattern

| # | Derivative | Channel | Lead time |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Original article | Newsletter / blog | D0 |
| 2 | LinkedIn long-form (3 hooks variants pick 1) | LinkedIn | D+1 |
| 3 | Twitter thread VN (5-7 tweets) | Twitter | D+2 |
| 4 | LinkedIn carousel (5-7 slides) | LinkedIn | D+3 |
| 5 | Newsletter "brief mention" snippet | Next newsletter | D+7 |
| 6 | YouTube short / talk segment (if recorded) | YouTube | D+14 |
| 7 | Quote graphics (1-3) | Twitter / IG | D+21 |

→ Reach lift target: ≥ 3× original.

## Engine Orchestration

| Step | Agent | Action |
|---|---|---|
| C4.1 | R-MKT | Within 24h of publish: generate `repurpose-plan.md` with 7 derivatives + dates. |
| C4.2 | R-CONTENT | Draft each derivative per channel template. |
| C4.3 | Human | Quick review per derivative (5 min each). |
| C4.4 | R-MKT | Schedule into calendar; track via cohort. |

## Outputs
```
projects/_business/marketing/repurpose/
└── {original-id}-repurpose-plan.md
```

## Definition of Done
- ✅ 7 derivatives planned within 24h of publish
- ✅ Each derivative is *channel-native* (not copy-paste)
- ✅ Reach 30-day total ≥ 3× original article reach
- ✅ Cohort tracked for lead attribution

## Failure Modes
- **Plan but no execute**: derivative count drops in week 2-3 → enforce calendar.
- **Lazy cross-post**: identical content → channel-native rewrite required.
- **Repurpose stale topic**: > 30 days since publish → fresh take or skip.
