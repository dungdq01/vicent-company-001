# Partnership / BD pipeline (BD0-BD4)

> Strategic alliances, channel partners, integrators, referral partners. Distinct from sales (S0-S5 = direct prospects) and content (C0-C4 = own audience).

**Owner**: R-BIZ + CEO sign + R-LEG (terms).
**Authority**: CEO mandatory at BD2 (terms) + BD4 (sunset).
**Cadence**: BD0-BD3 sequential per partner; BD4 quarterly review.

---

## Partnership types covered

| Type | Description | Revenue model |
|---|---|---|
| **Referral** | Informal lead exchange (e.g., friendly agency cross-refers) | % of closed deal (typically 10-20%) |
| **Co-sell** | Joint pitch to mutual prospects | Split per agreed terms (typically 50/50 on joint deals) |
| **Integration** | Technical alliance — embed studio's agent into partner platform | License fee OR rev-share OR usage-based |
| **Reseller** | Partner sells studio services as their own | Studio price floor; partner margin (typically 20-30%) |

---

## Phase Index

| # | Phase | Owner | Trigger | Output |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| BD0 | Identify | R-BIZ + CEO | Quarterly proactive scan OR opportunistic signal | Ranked candidate list |
| BD1 | Outreach | R-BIZ + CEO sign | Top 3 candidates from BD0 | Initial calls + interest signal |
| BD2 | Terms | CEO + R-LEG | Mutual interest from BD1 | Signed partnership agreement |
| BD3 | Execute | R-BIZ + R-MKT | Terms signed | Joint GTM live + tracking |
| BD4 | Review | CEO + COO + partner | Quarterly | Continue / renegotiate / sunset |
