```
**Original location**: knowledge/data/...
**Quarantined to**: knowledge/_quarantine/{slug}-{date}/
**Why flagged**: 1-2 sentence root cause + evidence link
**Affected projects**: list of project IDs with version_pin to this version
**Resolution path**: fix-and-republish | archive-permanent
**Resolution deadline**: 30 days from flag date
**Resolved at**: (filled when complete)
**Final disposition**: republished v{n+1} | archived
```
