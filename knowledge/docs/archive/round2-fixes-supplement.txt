```markdown
## STEP 0 — SCORE VALIDATION (chạy TRƯỚC mọi Tier)

⚠️ Round 1 scores có thể inaccurate — γ scoring formula không consistent.
Trước khi bắt đầu bất kỳ Tier nào, γ phải quick-validate ALL 6 "No Re-Run" modules.

PROCESS:
  1. γ đọc feasibility-report.md của từng module (6 modules)
  2. Extract raw dimension scores (Tech, Market, Data, Risk)
  3. Recalculate overall với CORRECT formula:
     Overall = Tech × 0.30 + Market × 0.30 + Data × 0.20 + (10 - Risk) × 0.20
  4. Apply verdict thresholds:
     GO: ≥ 7.0 AND no dimension < 5
     CONDITIONAL GO: 5.0–6.9
     NO-GO: < 5.0

OUTCOMES:
  - Score vẫn ≥ 7.0 → confirm GO, không re-run
  - Score drops to 6.8–6.9 → promote to Tier C (re-score)
  - Score drops to 6.0–6.7 → promote to Tier B (targeted re-research)
  - Score drops < 6.0 → promote to Tier A (full re-research)

Update round-2-research-plan.md MODULE ASSIGNMENTS nếu có promotions.

DURATION: ~30 min total (5 min per module, score validation only)
AGENT: γ only. σ NOT needed at this step.
```

### Fix 3: 🟠 Thêm Scoring Calibration Reference

**Location:** Thêm sau MODULE ASSIGNMENTS section

```markdown
## SCORING CALIBRATION

γ re-scoring trong Round 2 PHẢI dùng calibration anchors để consistent across modules.

REFERENCES:
  - Weights + thresholds: R-γ skill card §Scoring Formula
  - Dimension anchors: xem bảng dưới đây

CALIBRATION ANCHORS (γ reference khi scoring):

Technical Feasibility (1-10):
  10: Mature, proven at scale, multiple production deployments
      Ví dụ: LightGBM for tabular prediction, YOLO for object detection
  7:  Production-ready nhưng cần expertise, vài gotchas
      Ví dụ: TFT for multi-horizon forecasting, RAG pipeline
  4:  Experimental, ít production cases, chưa stabilize
      Ví dụ: Physics-Informed Neural Networks, autonomous agents
  1:  Purely theoretical, chưa có implementation viable

Market Demand (1-10):
  10: Every enterprise needs this, active buying cycle
      Ví dụ: RAG/chatbot, demand forecasting, fraud detection
  7:  Strong demand trong specific verticals, growing market
      Ví dụ: Computer Vision cho manufacturing, OCR cho logistics
  4:  Niche demand, early adopters only, market chưa educated
      Ví dụ: Knowledge Graph standalone, digital twin for SMEs
  1:  No clear buyer, solution looking for problem

Data Availability (1-10):
  10: Client luôn có data sẵn, quality tốt, volume đủ
      Ví dụ: Transaction data (retail), network logs (telecom)
  7:  Data có nhưng cần cleaning/integration, medium volume
      Ví dụ: Medical records (structured nhưng siloed)
  4:  Data sparse, expensive to collect, privacy constraints
      Ví dụ: Agricultural sensor data, rare disease datasets
  1:  Hầu như không có data, phải build from scratch

Implementation Risk (1-10, 10 = RISKIEST):
  10: Extremely risky — novel tech, no precedent, regulatory minefield
  7:  Significant risk — complex integration, data quality unknown
  4:  Moderate risk — proven approach, manageable complexity
  1:  Very low risk — well-established, plug-and-play solutions

Note: Risk is INVERTED in formula: Risk_inv = 10 - Risk_score.
High risk (10) → Risk_inv = 0 → drags overall DOWN.
Low risk (1) → Risk_inv = 9 → boosts overall UP.
```

### D.0 Trigger Conditions (EXPANDED)

     Ví dụ: B06 score 8.4 nhưng muốn thêm SOTA mới, deep hơn
     Scope: Thường Tier B hoặc Tier D (single-agent)

     Ví dụ: 6 tháng sau, cần update B09 Generative AI với latest models
     Scope: α re-research → σ re-consolidate (Tier B)
     
  d) TARGETED UPDATE — Chỉ muốn 1 agent cụ thể re-research
     Ví dụ: Muốn R-MLE re-research B01 từ góc ML Engineer mới
     Scope: Tier D (single-agent)
     
  e) SPOT CHECK — Verify quality bất kỳ lúc nào
     Ví dụ: Trước khi demo cho client, muốn kiểm tra B01 quality
     Scope: Chạy MODULE-TEST-PROTOCOL only, trigger Tier nếu fail
     
  f) CROSS-MODULE UPDATE — Insight từ module khác ảnh hưởng module này
     Ví dụ: B13 research phát hiện technique applicable cho B01
     Scope: α update cross-domain section → σ update (Tier D)

KHÔNG dùng Workflow D khi:
  - Module chưa có Round 1 output → dùng Workflow A
  - Chỉ cần fix typo/formatting → Manager direct edit, không cần workflow

MANAGER CÓ TOÀN QUYỀN:
  - Trigger Workflow D cho bất kỳ module nào, bất kỳ lúc nào
  - Chọn bất kỳ scope nào (Tier A / B / C / D)
  - Chọn bất kỳ agent nào để re-run
  - Không cần justify bằng score hay test failure
